Case Study

Water Quality in the Netherlands

Spatial and seasonal analysis of Dutch water measurements, turning raw samples into decision-ready insights for monitoring teams.

Approach

  • Merged multi-region datasets; cleaned and standardized fields with NumPy/Pandas
  • Feature engineered seasonal and location-based flags, then ran variance and trend analysis
  • Built anomaly checks to surface stations needing rapid follow-up testing

How it helps local teams

  • Water boards and utilities can prioritize sampling routes by province and month
  • Pet owners, aquaculture, and recreation groups can quickly see if conditions are acceptable
  • Provides exportable summaries and clear visuals for reporting and stakeholder updates

Technologies

  • Python (NumPy, Pandas, Matplotlib/Seaborn)
  • Jupyter Notebook for rapid exploration and storytelling
  • Data quality checks, anomaly detection heuristics, and presentation-ready charts
