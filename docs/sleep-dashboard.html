Case Study

Sleep Pattern & Health Insights Dashboard

Deeply personal project to quantify lifelong sleep issues using Fitbit/Apple Health data, and surface actions that improve rest.

Data ingestion

  • Fitbit/Apple Health exports: sleep stages (light/REM/deep), overnight heart rate, HRV
  • Bedtime/wake-time consistency, screen-time proxies, and tags for interventions (new pillow, evening workouts)
  • Synthetic data support to mirror Fitbit format for safe demos

Analysis with Pandas

  • Sleep duration vs. bedtime consistency and evening screen-time
  • HRV and overnight heart-rate drift for recovery vs. stress signals
  • “Bad night → next day” effects: steps, focus/mood proxies, and workout intensity
  • Weekly/monthly trends to spot patterns by weekday vs. weekend

Modeling & detection

  • Classification: predict good vs. bad nights
  • Regression: predict total sleep duration given behavior signals
  • Anomaly detection: flag unusual patterns that could correlate with apnea-like events

Dashboard & visuals

  • Heatmaps of sleep stages, circadian rhythm charts, and weekly stacks for instant scanning
  • Run sheet highlighting interventions that move the needle (e.g., bedtime regularity)
  • Exportable summaries to share with clinicians or trainers

How I frame it in interviews

I turned a lifelong sleep problem into a data pipeline: clean → combine → analyze → present. The project balances technical rigor with empathy—data supports decisions, but it never tells the whole story.
