1. Sleep Pattern & Health Insights Dashboard

Built an end-to-end pipeline for Fitbit/Apple Health exports to understand lifelong sleep issues. Cleaned and merged night-level data (sleep stages, heart rate, HRV, bedtime consistency), then explored:

  • Sleep duration vs. bedtime consistency and screen-time proxies
  • HRV and nightly heart-rate patterns mapped against perceived restfulness
  • “Good vs. bad night” classifiers and duration regression baselines
  • Weekly/monthly trend dashboards plus anomaly flags for possible apnea-like events

2. Water Quality in the Netherlands

Combined regional water measurements to spot seasonal and spatial risk in Dutch rivers. Used NumPy/Pandas for cleaning, feature engineering, and QC before building visuals that water boards can use for monitoring:

  • Trend and variance analysis by province and month to prioritize sampling
  • Anomaly detection to flag stations for rapid follow-up testing
  • Exportable summaries so local teams (pet owners, aquaculture, utilities) can check suitability quickly

3. YouTube Recommendation Bias & Viewing Behavior Model

Built a “baby YouTube algorithm” informed by my own watch history (exported JSON/HTML). Mapped videos as nodes and transitions as edges to study how recommendations shape viewing paths.

  • Analyzed watch time by category, channel return rates, and topic transitions over years
  • Graph-based recommendation simulator estimating “likely next video” probabilities
  • Topic clustering to find core interest groups and detect binge vs. casual sessions
  • Metrics for topic drift and binge likelihood grounded in personal viewing patterns
